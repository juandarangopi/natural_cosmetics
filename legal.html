Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At Natural Cosmetics, we want to ensure customer satisfaction. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may request a return under the following conditions.

01 Return Request Period

Customers may request a return within the first 7 calendar days after receiving the order.

02 Conditions for Accepting Returns

To be eligible for a return:

  • The product must be unused.
  • The product must be in its original packaging.
  • The product must not show signs of damage, alteration, or improper handling.
  • All original accessories or included items must be returned.

For hygiene and safety reasons, we do not accept returns of opened or used cosmetic products.

03 Damaged or Incorrect Products

If you received a defective, damaged, or incorrect product, please contact us within the first 48 hours after delivery.

You will need to provide:

  • Order number.
  • Photos of the product.
  • Description of the issue.
04 Refunds

Once the return is approved, the refund will be processed using the same payment method used for the purchase.

Processing times may vary depending on the bank or payment platform.

05 Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the error was caused by Natural Cosmetics.

06 Non-Refundable Products

Returns or refunds are not accepted for:

  • Opened or used products.
  • Products purchased during special promotions or discounts.
  • Products damaged due to misuse.
07 Order Cancellations

Orders may only be canceled before they are shipped.

Once the order has been dispatched, it must be processed as a return.
